Descle Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-11

1. Overview

Descle is a Chrome extension that replaces the new tab page with a dashboard displaying your schedule, todos, and bookmarks. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and how it is stored.

2. Data We Access

PermissionData AccessedPurpose
identityGoogle account email, profile imageGoogle Calendar authentication
identity (OAuth)Google Calendar events (read-only)Display today's schedule
bookmarksChrome bookmark titles and URLsDisplay bookmarks in the dashboard
storageUser-created todos, preferencesPersist settings across sessions
alarmsNonePeriodic calendar data refresh

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). No data is transmitted to or stored on any external server.

4. Third-Party Services

Descle communicates only with Google's API servers to retrieve your calendar data. This communication occurs directly between your browser and Google. We do not operate any intermediary servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not collect, sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you:

  • Clear the extension's storage
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Disconnect your Google account from the extension

7. User Control

  • You can disconnect your Google account at any time via the "Disconnect" button.
  • You can delete individual todos or bookmarks directly in the dashboard.
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revision date at the top of the page will be changed. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.
